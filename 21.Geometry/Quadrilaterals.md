# Quadrilaterals

Quadrilateral is a polygon with 4 edges

Rectangle: 4 edges, 4 vertices, 4 right angles; width: $w$, height: $h$; Area $A=w*h$; Perimeter $p=2*(w+h)$

Square: reactangle when w=h 

Trapezium: 4 edge, 4 vertices, one pair of parallel edges.

length of edges: w1,w2; geight: h 
area= 0.5*(w1+w2)*h 

Parallelogram is a polygon with four edges and four vertices. Moreover, the opposite sides must be parallel.

8. Kite is a quadrilateral which has two pairs of sides of the same length which are adjacent to each other. The area of a kite is diagonal 1 × diagonal 2 /2.

9. Rhombus is a special parallelogram where every side has equal length. It is also a special case of kite where every side has equal length.
